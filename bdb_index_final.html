What is Lighthouse

Lighthouse is developed by engineers to engineers providing common resource of useful commands and support links to help in troubleshooting.

This portal is developed for engineers to edit/add or delete resources. Once submitted, the new content will be reviewed by admins to validate and check accuracy before it becomes available to all users.

The resources (commands and links) are grouped by Platform/sub-Technology.

Commands section is intended to provide helpful commands or tools for troubleshooting.

Links section is intended to provide helpful links or BU alias for further investigation. Helpful resources troubleshooting could be tech-zone, Cisco Wikis, XR-geeks link , webex recording, topic or any Cisco documents.
